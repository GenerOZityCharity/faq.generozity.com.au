FAQ

General Questions

What is GenerOZity? What is a Guerilla Popup Charity? Guerilla means we are independent and small. We're not affiliated with any single other charity, and raise funds are a large number of causes.
For more information, visit https://charity.generozity.com.au/

How much of the money raised do you give away? How do you cover operating costs? 100% of donations using our portal are given to the promoted causes (GenerOZity has raised over $115,000, and given away every cent).
Our operating costs are covered by sponsorships, and we've continued to work hard to kept those costs low (Thanks to our large group of volunteers).

Who runs GenerOZity? Who has access to Generozity funds? GenerOZity is run by the 4 current members of the board of GenerOZity Limited (Joel Rennie, Austin Adamson, Ashley Hull, and Kevin Eidelman).
Only two members have access to the bank accounts, and hold all financial responsibilities (Approval from both are required to move any money).

What charities has GenerOZity raised money for? Childsplay Charity, Checkpoint Organisation, Cure Cancer Australia, Fire & Rescue Services (RFS/CFA). GenerOZity publishes a record of the money raised on our blog.

Is GenerOZity a charity with deductible gift recipient (DGR) status? As of January 2020, GenerOZity Limited is a Not-for-profit organisation - not an entity that can offer tax deductible receipts on donations.
We are working with the ACNC to become a registered Charity in the next few months.

I have a problem with generozity. What can I do? What is your grievance policy? Please contact the GenerOZity team directly by email or in person at the event. Please request multiple staff members if required
